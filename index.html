Welcome! Thanks for stopping by.

In April, 2016 I decided to start on a focused learning path. I've been taking massive online open courses (MOOCs) for several years now, but there has not been a common thread or goal tying the courses together. Furthermore, the classes did not inspire much work beyond the requirements for course completion.

My current long-term goal is to complete a website I've been thinking about for a couple of years. To build the skills necessary to achieve this goal, I'll be doing coursework and small side projects. You can track my progress on the goals page. You can also see the results of some of my side projects in the blog section.

The primary purpose of this site is to serve as a reference for myself and those encouraging me on this journey, but I hope others find some value in it as well.
